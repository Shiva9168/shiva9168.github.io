About Me

Who Am I?

Hey, I’m Shiva! I’m a passionate Game Developer and a student from India. I’ve been creating games using Unreal Engine and learning everything from 3D modeling and animating to sculpting. While most of my games are private, I love sharing tips and tutorials on YouTube to help others get into Game Development.

I’m also pretty experienced with tools like Unreal Engine, Blender, and Photoshop, and I’m learning C++ to level up my skills. Apart from Game Dev, I know Python, JavaScript, Node.js, React.js, and more, thanks to my time exploring web development. When I’m not working, I’m gaming—because games are what inspire me the most. My goal? To make amazing games and share them with the world!
